About ICNIIS 2026

The International Conference on Next-Gen Integrated and Intelligent Systems (ICNIIS 2026) aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to share their innovative ideas and research in the domains of Electronics, VLSI, Embedded Systems, AI/ML, IoT, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Smart Networks. The conference will serve as a platform for discussing the convergence of emerging technologies driving Intelligent Automation, Next-Generation Electronics, and Digital Infrastructure.

Aligned with national missions like Semicon India, Digital India, and Atmanirbhar Bharat, this conference promotes indigenous innovation and future-ready technology development in intelligent systems and integrated electronic solutions.

Electronics & VLSI

Advanced semiconductor technologies and electronic system design for next-generation applications.

AI/ML Integration

Artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions for intelligent automation systems.

IoT & Networks

Internet of Things and smart network architectures for connected intelligent systems.

Cyber-Physical Systems

Integration of computational algorithms and physical components in modern systems.

Join the Future of Technology

Be part of the conversation shaping the next generation of integrated and intelligent systems.
